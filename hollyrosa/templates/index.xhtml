Need Access to Hollyrosa?

You will need a login to access Hollyrosa and your account needs to be active.

Accounts are usually deactivated after a time of inactivity.

The program PL, Mackan or Valdemar can typically help with login, activation and password-reset. If you don't know who they are, the system is probably not for you.
